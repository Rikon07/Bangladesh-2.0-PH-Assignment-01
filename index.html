Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation fuels creativity and progress, introducing new ideas, technologies, and solutions that transform industries and improve lives globally.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures balanced development, resources, protecting ecosystems & environmental, social-economic well-being for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure forms the backbone of development, connectivity, supporting economic activities & enhancing quality of life through robust systems & facilities.

Empowerment

Empowerment uplifts the communities, providing confidence, opportunities & enabling active participation in decision-making & growth.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity, streamline operations, improve efficiency, foster innovation, bridge digital divides, and provide equitable access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Green energy harnesses renewable resources to produce clean, sustainable power. It reduces greenhouse gas emissions, combats climate change & promotes environmental preservation for a healthier planet.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure integrates advanced technology & sustainable design to enhance connectivity, efficiency & resilience. It supports economic growth, improves quality of life & addresses contemporary challenges.
